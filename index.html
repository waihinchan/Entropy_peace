The game rules:

You can set 2-10 plays which depends on how many people will play.

Victory condition:

You can produce money by set three different architectures. After 10 minutes of the game, the one with the most money wins.

Failure condition:

When generating money, it will also produce CO2. All players share one CO2 HP. When the CO2 HP value drops to 0, all players collectively fail.
